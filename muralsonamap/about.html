Murals on a Map (v1)

A hobby project to map out murals in Utrecht, NL. This first version took a lot of manual labor, especially the resizing of photos :-). The images I use are of poor quality, I scaled them to max 300px. That is on purpose: the best way to enjoy these murals is to be in front of them. So enjoy this site as your guide for a nice bike ride :). Please leave feedback/suggestions at my Reddit post or as an issue at the Github repo.
